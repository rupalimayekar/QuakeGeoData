Earthquake Data Analysis

The USGS is responsible for providing scientific data about natural hazards, the health of our ecosystems and environment; and the impacts of climate and land-use change. Their scientists develop new methods and tools to supply timely, relevant, and useful information about the Earth and its processes. This website shows the USGS earthquake data using some interractive visualizations that you can play around with to view the information you like. The maps show earthquakes occurring in the last 7 days.
